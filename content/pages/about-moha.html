About Mother of Hope Arusha Philosophicum (MOHA)

The Mission: The Cross and the Logos

The Mother of Hope Arusha Philosophicum is the foundational house of studies for the **Passionist Fathers (C.P.)** in East Africa, forming future priests for the Vice-Province across **Kenya, Tanzania, and Mozambique**. Our mission, rooted in the charism of **St. Paul of the Cross**, is twofold: to contemplate the boundless love of God revealed in the **Passion of Jesus Christ** and to preach it as the world‚Äôs most urgent need.

MOHA provides the intellectual formation necessary for ministerial effectiveness, guided by the principle: **"To know the truth of things"** (St. Thomas Aquinas).

Our Intellectual Tradition: Thomistic Philosophy

As a philosophical seminary, MOHA provides comprehensive academic training centered on the classical tradition of **Thomistic Philosophy**. Our studies are firmly grounded in the thought of **St. Thomas Aquinas**, the Angelic Doctor, alongside his philosophical predecessors, **Aristotle** and **Plato**.

Key Areas of Study:

  • **Metaphysics:** The study of Being and the attributes of God.
  • **Ethics and Moral Philosophy:** Laying the groundwork for sound moral theology and pastoral leadership.
  • **Epistemology:** The nature and certainty of human knowledge.
  • **African Philosophy:** Critical engagement with local wisdom and anthropologies to articulate the Faith within the cultural context of East Africa.

This rigorous intellectual formation ensures our priests are equipped to preach the crucified Christ to a world hungry for truth, especially within the diverse cultures of our region.

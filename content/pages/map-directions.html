?? Find Us: Mother of Hope Arusha Philosophicum

   
     

          The Mother of Hope Arusha Philosophicum is situated at the foot of Mount Meru, a place of peace and contemplation that perfectly complements our study of the Logos and the Passion. We are proud to be a vibrant part of the Arusha community, fostering vocations for the Passionist Vice-Province across Kenya, Tanzania, and Mozambique.      

 
 
     

Our Location and Contact Information

     

Institution Name: Mother of Hope Arusha Philosophicum (MOHA)

     

Physical Location: Njiro, Arusha, Tanzania
